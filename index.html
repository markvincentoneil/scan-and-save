Scan a barcode, get the product name, then search flyers and deals.

A newer version of this app is ready.

Hold the phone 10–14 inches (25–35 cm) from the barcode. Auto-scan captures when sharp, or tap Scan now anytime.

Camera blocked? iPhone: Settings → Safari → Camera → Allow, then reload this page. Also check Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → Safari is on.
